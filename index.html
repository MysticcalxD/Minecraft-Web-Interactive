About Us

What is Minecraft

Minecraft is a sandbox video game originally create by Mojang. There are two main gamemodes of this game, Creative and Survival. In Survival you need to survive, battling monsters just like real life before technology. In Creative you have unlimited blocks, hearts and health.

Platforms

The platforms Minecraft Java Edition can run on are Windows, MacOS and Linux. The platforms Minecraft Bedrock Edition can run on are Windows, Android and iOS/ipadOS. The platforms Minecraft Education Edition can run on are Windows, MacOS, iOS/ipadOS and Chrome OS.

Skins

In minecraft Skins means character. So that means you could change your skin whenever you want. But you need to buy Minecraft for that. Using Skindex you could get a skin or make your own one. Press the word skindex to visit the site.

Concept

Minecraft Suvival is basically surviving in real life without technology but you need to battle monstors at night. Creative you could do whatever you want without taking the hard way. Both of them could do the same things but not in the same ways.

The Nether

The nether is a place where you find most of the materials to defeat the end. The mobs in the nether are Blaze, Ghast, Hoglin, Magma, Cube, Piglin, Piglin Brute, Strider, Strider, Wither Skeleton, Zoglin and zombified piglins.

The End

The End is where everything ends. You need to go defeat the ender dragon with all the progress you made so far. You can find the Shulker, Endermans and the Ender dragon(of course). You can spawn the ender dragon if you defeat it.
